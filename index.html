🏡 1. What Is a Home Loan?

A home loan is a secured loan provided by banks/NBFCs to help you:

  • Buy a new or resale flat/house
  • Construct a house
  • Renovate your existing home
  • Buy a plot (with/without construction)

✅ 2. Home Loan Eligibility Criteria

CriteriaSalariedSelf-employed
Age21 to 60 years21 to 65 years
Employment TypeGovt/Private EmployeesProfessionals, Business Owners
Minimum Monthly Income₹25,000–₹30,000ITRs showing sufficient income
Work Experience2–3 years3 years in business
Credit Score700+ (recommended)
Loan Amount75% to 90% of property value

📂 3. Required Documents

For Salaried:

  • PAN & Aadhaar
  • 3–6 Months Salary Slips
  • 2–3 Years Form 16
  • 6 Months Bank Statements
  • Employment Letter, Address Proof

For Self-employed:

  • PAN & Aadhaar
  • GST/Trade License
  • 3 Years ITR & Computation
  • CA Certified Balance Sheet
  • Bank Statements, Business Proof

For Property:

  • Sale Agreement
  • Title Deed & Plan Approval
  • NOC if required

📋 4. Types of Home Loans

  • Home Purchase Loan
  • Plot + Construction Loan
  • Construction Loan
  • Home Improvement Loan
  • Balance Transfer
  • Top-Up Loan

💰 5. Loan Amount & LTV Ratio

  • Up to ₹30 lakh: 90%
  • ₹30–75 lakh: 80%
  • Above ₹75 lakh: 75%

🧲 6. EMI Calculation

EMI depends on loan amount, tenure (up to 30 years), and interest rate (8.35% to 10.5%).

🏦 7. Home Loan Process

  1. Check Eligibility & CIBIL
  2. Choose Bank (SBI, HDFC, ICICI, etc.)
  3. Apply & Submit Documents
  4. Verification by Bank
  5. Receive Sanction Letter
  6. Property Legal & Valuation Check
  7. Loan Agreement & Disbursement

🧠 8. Tips for Easy Approval

  • Maintain 750+ CIBIL
  • Clear debts & credit card dues
  • Add co-applicant if needed
  • Ensure complete & clean documents

📉 9. Common Charges

  • Processing Fee: 0.25% to 1%
  • Legal/Valuation: ₹5,000–₹10,000
  • Prepayment: NIL for floating
  • EMI Bounce: ₹500–750

📅 10. Top Banks (2025)

  • SBI: 8.35% - No hidden charges
  • LIC HFL: 8.45% - Long tenure
  • HDFC: 8.50% - Quick process
  • ICICI: 8.55% - Digital process
  • PNB Housing: 8.75% - Flexible terms
