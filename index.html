Client Education Corner

1. What is a Home Loan?

A secured loan used to purchase, construct or renovate a house.

  • Buy Flat/House
  • Renovation or Construction
  • Purchase Plot with/without construction

2. Eligibility Criteria

CriteriaSalariedSelf-employed
Age21-6021-65
Income₹25,000+3yr ITRs
Credit Score700+ recommended
LTV75%-90% of value

3. Documents Required

  • PAN, Aadhaar, Salary Slips / ITRs
  • Bank Statement, Property Papers
  • Title Deed, Sale Agreement, NOC

4. Types of Loans

  • Home Purchase / Top-up Loan
  • Construction / Improvement Loan
  • Plot + Construction

5. EMI & Charges

Rates between 8.35%–10.5%, tenure up to 30 years.

  • Processing: 0.25–1%
  • Legal/Valuation: ₹5K–₹10K
  • Prepayment: Free (floating)
